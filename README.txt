------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KV Airlines - The Program
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Introduction - A C++ Program to Simulate a Flight Reservation Program.
No. of Lines - 5850 (Including Headers and Docstrings).

Features     - 0) View Manifest - Staff Only
                  View Manifest Records of various flights on various days, customisable to requirements.
               1) Book a Flight
                  Book a Flight, Providing various details which are asked in the program. 
                  The program then allocates seats for you in the database, saves your input,
                  and generates E-Tickets (.doc files) for your use at the Airport.
               2) Manage your Booking
                  Manage your booking, by choosing any of the available options:
                  -> View the booking summary.
                  -> Cancel few/all bookings.
                  -> Change the Date and Seat of Travel.
                  -> View Passenger Data in Detail.
                  -> Redownload E-Tickets.
               3) View Time-Table of Flights
                  View the Flight Timings for each day, with a brief description.
               4) Leave Feedback 
                  Leave some feedback for the developer.
               5) View Customer Helpline Details
                  Displays the customer details, along with a nice picture.

Graphics     - Win32API only.
